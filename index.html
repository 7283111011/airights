Enforceable rights and meaningful protection, in the age of artificial intelligence.

FAIR is a UK charity working on how artificial intelligence should be governed in this country. We produce research and written evidence for Parliament and the regulators, and we publish plain-English material on the law as it applies to AI.

“I’ve spent most of my career making sure people in clinical trials are protected by enforceable rules. AI should be held to the same standard.”
Scott McCulloch · Founder, FAIR
