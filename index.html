The Foundation for Artificial Intelligence Rights

Artificial intelligence is being built into the country around you. We are here to help you understand it, push back on it, and demand it works for you.

FAIR is a new United Kingdom organisation with two jobs. The first is to help people, including children, teenagers, students, working adults, older adults, families and carers, to understand what artificial intelligence is, what it can and cannot do, and what their rights are when a public service, school, employer or application uses it. The second is to hold the people building and buying these systems to account: in Whitehall, in Parliament, in regulators’ offices, in NHS trusts, in council chambers, and in police forces.
