Three things, done well.

The Foundation works on three fronts — a public register, parliamentary submissions, and a safe channel for citizens to tell us where artificial intelligence has been used on them.

Watch the contracts.

A free public register of every confirmed artificial intelligence contract, partnership and pilot involving a United Kingdom public body. Scanned daily. Every entry sourced.

Open AI Alerts →

Push the law.

Independent, evidence-led submissions to Parliament, regulators and Government on the harms with the clearest evidence and the strongest public mandate.

Read the research →

Know your rights.

A safe and structured channel for people in the United Kingdom to tell the Foundation when artificial intelligence has been used on them. Anonymous by default.

The Foundation

Built for public accountability.

Artificial intelligence is being built into the country around you. FAIR exists to help you understand it, push back on it, and demand it works for you.

Independent. Evidence-led. United Kingdom focused.

“Most of my career, I have supported drug development by helping organisations navigate complex regulatory requirements, ensuring patient safety and rights are upheld whilst generating data of the highest integrity.” Scott McCulloch · Founder, FAIR
