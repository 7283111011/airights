Public understanding, enforceable rights, and meaningful protection in the age of artificial intelligence.

FAIR is a UK charity advancing public understanding of artificial intelligence and the rights it engages, and supporting better-informed decisions on how AI is developed, deployed, and governed. We provide research, briefings, and evidence to Parliament, the regulators, and the wider professional community on the legal, ethical, and societal questions AI now raises.

“I have spent most of my career working to ensure that the rights of patients are upheld in clinical trials. The regulations governing that space are explicitly designed to safeguard human rights. AI can learn from this; indeed, it must.”
Scott McCulloch · Founder, FAIR
