Enforceable rights and genuine accountability in the age of artificial intelligence.

FAIR is a UK charity working to ensure that artificial intelligence is developed, deployed, and governed within a framework of enforceable rights, transparent accountability, and meaningful protection for the public. We engage Parliament, the regulators, and the wider professional community on the legislative reforms the UK now requires.

“I have spent most of my career working to ensure that the rights of patients are upheld in clinical trials. The regulations governing that space are explicitly designed to safeguard human rights. AI can learn from this; indeed, it must.”
Scott McCulloch · Founder, FAIR
