Engage with policy and regulators across the whole AI landscape

Shaping AI policy and regulation across every domain where AI affects the public.

Project Lead
To be appointed on formation of the Board
Status
In planning
Workstream
Core function

While our copyright and IP project focuses on creators, this project takes a wider view: shaping policy and regulation across every domain where AI affects the public. Unchecked rollout brings real risks; this is the workstream that engages with regulators, government departments and industry bodies to ensure those risks are surfaced, debated and addressed.
