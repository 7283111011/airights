Newsletter.

FAIR Briefings: a short, plain-English read on UK artificial-intelligence policy and your rights, sent roughly every two weeks.

FAIR Briefings is a plain-English newsletter on UK artificial-intelligence policy and rights. It is sent roughly every two weeks, it is free, and it carries no tracking.

Hosted by Buttondown. See our privacy notice.

What to expect

  • Cadence: roughly fortnightly, usually on a Friday.
  • Length: short, around 700 to 1,000 words.
  • Format: plain-text email, with no tracking pixels.
  • Content: open consultations and their deadlines, court judgments, regulator decisions, brief notes on FAIR’s work, and selected reading.
  • Cost: free.
  • Unsubscribe: one click, on any issue.

Your data

We use your email address only to send the newsletter, and it is processed by Buttondown as our data processor on the lawful basis of consent. We do not share or sell it, and you can withdraw consent at any time using the unsubscribe link in any issue. See our privacy notice for details.
