Briefings

FAIR briefings are short, plain-English position papers on questions of UK AI policy, law, and rights. They are published in furtherance of FAIR’s charitable purposes (advancement of education and the advancement of human rights as engaged by AI), and are written for parliamentarians, civil servants, regulators, journalists, and the wider public.
