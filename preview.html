Scott McCulloch

Founder, FAIR

Scott has spent close to two decades advising drug-development organisations on the regulatory frameworks that protect patients in clinical trials. He is in the final stages of an LLB at the University of Law, with postgraduate study in AI Law at the University of Oxford.

The Foundation grew directly out of his final LLB research project, which examined whether United Kingdom intellectual property law had been adapted to the use of artificial intelligence. The conclusion was that it had not.
